About Us

Manav Seva Dal is an organization of selfless, selfless, dedicated and disciplined volunteers, who rise above differences of caste, sectarian, religious and spiritual fanaticism, considers human service as their worship, worship and yajan. He is sensitive not only to humans, but also to animals. They are always ready, dynamic and determined to do every kind of service as much as possible. Such is the one who has an innate nature, pride, satisfaction and is imbued with social, secular, national feelings and spiritual qualities, only an organization of volunteers full of such sensitive and analytical personality is truly selfless in the service of human beings and beings. may be moving forward.

Service towards living beings is that active expression of love for which there is no personal desire of a social worker because selfless service is the emotional gateway to mental peace, self-knowledge, conduct-satisfaction and self-realization.

For the complete fulfillment of the objectives of the party, the goal of all the members should be "selfless dedicated personality". Ideal leadership and morality, etc., naturally manifest various attractive wonderful qualities in the workers with selfless dedicated personality. With a dedicated personality, there will always be freshness and enthusiasm in the organization. The selfless dedicated personality is the pride of the organization and its character mirror.

This is my message to all the volunteers of Manav Seva Dal that following the manual written in the book, pave such a creative path of selfless service, whose reflection will automatically affect the society and for the creation of an ideal society, the effect of clean ideology should be continuously in motion. Being service-minded people will be attracted to serve in the organization and surely the power of the hardworking volunteers will increase.

Therefore, the members of the organization should present a golden example of social service, which paves the way for selfless service for the future.

Shri Satpal Ji Maharaj
